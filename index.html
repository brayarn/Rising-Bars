The goal is to keep the ball within the game canvas as long as possible. The game level changes during gameplay and the speed of the bars increases with every level.

On mobile devices:

Long press either side of the game canvas to move the ball left or right accordingly

On personal computers:

Using the left or right arrow keys to move the ball to the left or right. Use escape button to pause.
